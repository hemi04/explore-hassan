Welcome to Hassan

Hassan District is a jewel of Karnataka, celebrated for its Hoysala-era architecture, peaceful pilgrim centers, and refreshing natural getaways. This portal showcases only the tourist places within Hassan District—helping you plan an enriching and responsible journey.

  • World-famous Hoysala temples at Belur and Halebidu
  • Spiritual serenity at Shravanabelagola
  • Photogenic Shettihalli Rosary Church ruins
  • Panoramic vistas from Manjarabad Fort
